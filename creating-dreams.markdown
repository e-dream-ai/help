<p class="body-text">
  Dreams should be 15 seconds long or more. 16x9 aspect ration and 1080p resolution or higher.
  No credits, titles, watermarks, bugs, fade-ins or outs. Just pure generations!
</p>
<p class="body-text">
  Almost any video format should work. Upload the highest quality and highest bitrate possible.
  Infinidream transcodes to high quality x265 (crf 24) for consistent playback.
</p>
